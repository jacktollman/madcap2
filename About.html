About

Toby and Dani are the co-founders and associate producers of RBL. They met whilst training at the Bristol Old Vic and Dani grew up in Reading. Making productions happen and being a small part of the drive to culturally embolden our town continues to prove a hugely rewarding experience for Dani and Toby.

Creative Directors

Alice Swire

Alice was born and raised in London. She graduated from Stella Adler in New York City in 2015 where she studied Theatre.

Her theatrical credits include Hurlybury, Peer Gynt, Twelve Angry Women, Loves Labours Lost and Sleep No More.

Upon returning to the UK she began working in film production and worked on several scripted dramas as part of the production team.

Her recent credits include Beecham House for ITV directed by Gurinder Chdaha (Bend it Like Beckham, Blinded By The Light) and Amulet directed by Romola Garai.
